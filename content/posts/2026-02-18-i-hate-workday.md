I hate using [Workday](https://www.workday.com/) to apply to companies. I can't speak to all the other things they offer, but the experience for job applicants sucks.

Why do I have to create a separate account for each company that uses Workday to handle applications? Why do you ask me to upload my resume and then still prompt me to manually enter data? Why is the application process so long compared to other tools like [Greenhouse](https://www.greenhouse.com/) and [Ashby](https://www.ashbyhq.com/)?

I swear I've avoided applying to so many companies just because they forced me to create a Workday account to apply. I'd rather stay unemployed and apply elsewhere.

Just look at how much people hate Workday. [Business Insider published an article](https://www.businessinsider.com/everyone-hates-workday-human-resources-customer-service-software-fortune-500-2024-5) about how much people dislike it. People on social media like Threads are [also annoyed](https://www.threads.com/@imjustnycole/post/DBxYoW3PVO_). The best place to see people frustrated with Workday is Reddit. Search for ["workday sucks site:reddit.com"](https://www.google.com/search?q=workday+sucks+site%3Areddit.com) on Google and you'll see what I mean. Titles like ["I fucking hate Workday."](https://www.reddit.com/r/recruitinghell/comments/1jywuzk/i_fucking_hate_workday/) tell you everything you need to know.

Fuck Workday.
